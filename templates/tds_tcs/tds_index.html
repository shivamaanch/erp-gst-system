About TDS (Tax Deducted at Source)

TDS is tax deducted by the payer on certain payments. Common TDS sections:
• Section 194C - Contract payments (2% above ₹10,000)
• Section 194J - Professional fees (10% above ₹30,000)
• Section 194I - Interest payments (10% above ₹5,000)
• Section 194A - Rent payments (10% above ₹15,000)
• Section 192A - Commission (2% above ₹50,000)

Important Note

TDS certificates must be issued to the deductee and filed with income tax returns.
Due dates: Monthly by 7th of following month.
