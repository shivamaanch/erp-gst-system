Income Tax Act Depreciation Rates:
  • Building: 10% (WDV Method)
  • Plant & Machinery: 15% (WDV Method)
  • Motor Vehicles: 15% (WDV Method)
  • Furniture & Fittings: 10% (WDV Method)
  • Computers & Software: 40% (WDV Method)
  • Intangible Assets: 25% (WDV Method)
  • Other Assets: 15% (WDV Method)
  • Note: Half rate for assets purchased after 180 days from year end
Important Notes:
  • Depreciation is calculated on Written Down Value (WDV) method as per IT Act
  • For new assets purchased after 180 days from year end, half rate applies
  • Block of assets concept applies - similar assets are grouped together
  • Sale proceeds are deducted from the block before calculating depreciation
  • If sale proceeds exceed WDV of block, short term capital gain arises
