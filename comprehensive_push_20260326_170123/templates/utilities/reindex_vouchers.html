Reindex All Vouchers

Warning

This will regenerate ALL voucher numbers for the current financial year. This action cannot be undone. Make sure you have a backup before proceeding.

What will happen:
  • All Sales invoices will be renumbered as SV/{{ session.get('fin_year') }}/0001, 0002, 0003...
  • All Purchase invoices will be renumbered as PV/{{ session.get('fin_year') }}/0001, 0002, 0003...
  • All Milk entries will be renumbered as MV/{{ session.get('fin_year') }}/0001, 0002, 0003...
  • All Journal entries will be renumbered as JV/{{ session.get('fin_year') }}/0001, 0002, 0003...
  • Numbering will be based on transaction date (oldest first)
